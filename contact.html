ABOUT US

Homerton College, part of the University of Cambridge, prides itself on fostering a welcoming and inclusive community that supports academic excellence and personal growth.
With a rich history and a modern outlook, the college offers top-notch facilities, including a well-stocked library, extensive sports amenities, and beautiful gardens.
Homerton is dedicated to providing a vibrant and stimulating environment where students can thrive both academically and socially, with a focus on holistic development and a strong sense of belonging.
